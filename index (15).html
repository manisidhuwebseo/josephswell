Answer Engine Optimisation and Generative Engine Optimisation for medical clinics. Get found when patients use AI search, voice search, and answer engines to find healthcare providers.

Healthcare Answer Engine Optimisation (AEO)

Healthcare AEO is the process of optimising your medical clinic's online content to appear in direct answers when patients ask questions to voice assistants (Siri, Google Assistant, Alexa) and answer engines. When patients ask "What are the symptoms of flu?" or "How do I find a GP near me?", healthcare AEO helps your medical practice become the featured answer.

  • Featured Snippets: Optimise healthcare content to appear in position zero on Google for patient questions
  • Voice Search Ready: Structure healthcare content for natural language voice queries from patients
  • FAQ Schema for Healthcare: Implement structured data so answer engines understand your medical content
  • Q&A Content Strategy: Create healthcare content that directly answers patient questions and concerns

Healthcare Generative Engine Optimisation (GEO)

Healthcare GEO focuses on optimising your medical clinic's content for generative AI engines like ChatGPT, Google SGE (Search Generative Experience), and Perplexity AI that generate direct answers for patient queries. With healthcare generative engine optimisation, your medical practice appears as a trusted source when AI generates responses to patient searches.

  • Healthcare Entity SEO: Establish your medical clinic as a recognised healthcare entity for AI knowledge graphs
  • Google SGE Optimisation: Prepare your healthcare content for AI-generated search results in Google
  • Healthcare Schema Markup: Use medical-specific structured data (MedicalEntity, Physician, Hospital) for AI understanding
  • Healthcare Authority Signals: Build citations and backlinks that AI trusts for medical information
